Commodities

Legumes

Legumes (Fabaceae ) are used both as food grain seed and livestock forage and silage. Legumes is the only vegetable culture which has a valuable vegetable protein which can substitute an animal protein. Legumes are rich in vitamins and microelements such as folate, manganese, thiamin, magnesium, iron. That is why legumes are largely used in vegetarian diet. Ukrainian legumes are represented by beans, soybeans, peas, lentils, lupins etc. Ukrainian legumes market is yet to develop and increase, since Ukrainian cuisine does not consume much legumes

Oil crops

Ukraine is one of the leading world’s exports of oil-bearing crops and their products. They include sunflower seeds which are a symbol of Ukraine, rapeseed, linaceae, corn, cannabaceae etc. The vegetable oil is healthy and full of essential elements and vitamins. Oil-bearing crops contain a triglyceride which is extracted from a plant as an oil. Vegetable oils are used by humans for centuries for food and fuel. Vegetable oil adds to culinary dishes its rich flavor. Vegetable oils are widely used in cosmetics and pharmaceutical industries (soaps, candles, skin products, perfumes, ointments etc.) In mechanics vegetable oil is a part of lubricants. Vegetable oil is an irreplaceable ingredient of pet food. It is also is used a fuel since ancient times

Grain

Grain is the most important vegetable plant in human history. In ancient times good grain harvest used to mean prosperity and happiness, while bad harvest of grains symbolized poverty and death. Ukraine is famous worldwide for its grain. Ukraine’s fertile soils, favorable climate conditions and cheap grain costs lead this country to the leaders of the world grain market. It is certain, that Ukraine does not use its full grain potential, so the harvest figures could be doubled in future in case of the improved management. Ukrainian grain is represented by wheat, rye, barley, millet, oats, buckwheat and others. The main problem of grain production in Ukraine is grain storage. Building new granaries and new transshipment complexes are among our primary plans
